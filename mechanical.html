Mechanical Quarterly Milestones

Q1 Milestones

Initial CAD Concept & Simulation

We successfully generated the first full systems architecture in SolidWorks. Initial FEA (Finite Element Analysis) simulations were conducted on the primary deployment joint to ensure a safety factor of 2.0 under wind loads.

Material Selection & BOM Finalization

After analyzing thermal expansion coefficients versus weight, we finalized 6061 aluminum alloy for the main structure. The Bill of Materials (BOM) was generated and vendors were selected for specialized components.

Q2 Milestones

Scale Prototype Fabrication

Our first 1:5 scale prototype was completed using 3D printed components (PLA). This allowed us to validate the kinematic analysis of the unfolding mechanism before moving to metal fabrication.

Actuator Integration Testing

The linear actuators were successfully mounted and integrated into the scale prototype. We measured power draw versus load to ensure the specified motors can handle operational stress.
