Name: Sera Ozkan

Major: Electrical Engineering

Description: I am an Electrical Engineering student at Seattle Pacific University pursuing a minor in Appropriate and Sustainable Engineering. My passion lies in power systems, renewable energy, and sustainable design, finding practical ways to make clean energy accessible and resilient. Through internships with Stantec and Tetra Tech, I’ve gained hands-on experience in power distribution, load calculations, Revit modeling, and renewable integration for commercial and mixed-use buildings. Beyond my internships, I serve as a board member of SPU’s IEEE student chapter, where I collaborate on robotics and microgrid research projects. I’ve also co-authored a paper on Airborne Wind Energy integration for microgrids, accepted for presentation at the IEEE PowerAfrica 2025 conference.
