Electrical Quarterly Milestones

Q1 Milestones

Core System Schematic & Power Budget

We completed the initial schematic for the master control board (MCU: ESP32). A rigorous power budget analysis was conducted, confirming that the 100W solar panel can successfully charge the 24V battery bank while driving both actuators simultaneously.

PCB Design & Component Selection

The main MCU PCB layout was finalized and sent for fabrication. Key components like the MPPT solar charge controller and motor drivers were selected and sourced, focusing on thermal performance and efficiency.

Q2 Milestones

MCU & Motor Driver Validation

The fabricated PCBs arrived and were successfully populated (SMD soldering). Initial code (C++) was deployed to test the ESP32’s control over the L298N motor drivers, validating PWM control for smooth actuator deployment.

Sensor Array Integration (Phase 1)

The solar irradiance sensor and temperature sensors were integrated via I2C to the MCU. Initial data logging scripts were validated, laying the groundwork for the SolUmbra tracking algorithm.
